Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my music?

Artists can upload their music through our Artist Portal. You'll need to create an artist account first, then follow the step-by-step upload process.

Is mymelody free to use?

Yes! mymelody offers a free tier with limited features. We also have premium plans that unlock additional features like offline listening and higher quality audio.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes! mymelody is available on both iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How do I create playlists?

To create a playlist, go to any song and click the "Add to playlist" button. You can create a new playlist or add to an existing one.
